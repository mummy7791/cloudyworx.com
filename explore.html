What is AssetOps?

AssetOps combines sensors, IoT, and machine learning with a robust EAM (Enterprise Asset Management) layer to monitor asset health, predict failures, and orchestrate work orders, parts, and field teams—end to end.

Core Capabilities

AI Monitoring

Stream, cleanse, and model telemetry from pumps, turbines, HVAC, fleets, and more to flag anomalies in real time.

Predictive Maintenance

Remaining useful life (RUL) models trigger preventive jobs before breakdowns occur.

Smart Work Orders

Auto-generate work orders with checklists, safety steps, and SLAs tied to asset criticality.

Inventory & Spares

Optimize reorder points and min/max levels using usage/lead-time forecasts.

Field Mobility

Offline apps for technicians with barcodes, photos, e-sign, and GPS time stamps.

Analytics & Compliance

MTBF/MTTR, downtime, parts burn, and audit trails for ISO/OSHA compliance.

Reference Architecture

  • Edge & IoT: Gateways, OPC-UA, Modbus, CAN bus, telematics.
  • Streaming: Time-series storage, rule engine, event router.
  • ML: Anomaly detection, forecasting, RUL, root cause models.
  • EAM: Assets, hierarchies, work orders, parts, vendors, SLA.
  • Apps: Web console, field mobility, customer/partner portals.
  • Security: SSO, RBAC, audit, encryption in transit/at rest.
